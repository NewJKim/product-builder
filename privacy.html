Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Nouri ("we," "our," or "the site") handles information when you visit this website.

Information We Collect

Nouri does not require you to create an account or submit personal information to use the calorie-tracking tool. All tracking data is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to or stored by us.

If you submit a message through the Partnership Inquiry form on our home page, your name, email address, and message are processed by Formspree, Inc. for delivery to our inbox. We do not independently store this data.

Cookies and Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its advertising partners use cookies to personalize ads based on your interests and browsing history. These cookies may track you across sites that use Google's advertising services.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out

For more information on how Google uses data from advertising, visit Google's partner site policy.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use Nouri. These services collect anonymized usage data such as pages visited, session duration, and device type. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Third-Party Services

Nouri integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense — advertising and cookie-based personalization
  • Google Analytics — anonymized usage analytics
  • Microsoft Clarity — session recording and heatmaps
  • Disqus — comment system on the Tracker page; subject to the Disqus Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — contact form processing; subject to the Formspree Privacy Policy

Data Retention

We do not directly collect or store personal data. Data processed by third-party services is subject to their respective retention and deletion policies.

Children's Privacy

Nouri is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Nouri after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Use the Partnership Inquiry form on our home page.
